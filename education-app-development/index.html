Frequently Asked Questions

How can you deliver education apps so fast?

Our AI-accelerated workflow automates repetitive coding, testing, and review tasks, allowing senior engineers to focus on architecture and complex features. What compliance standards do you follow for education apps?

We design apps to meet FERPA for student data privacy, WCAG 2.2 for accessibility, and SOC 2 for operational security. For mobile apps, we adhere to OWASP MASVS guidelines. Our team can also support COPPA and CCPA requirements as needed.

Can you integrate with existing LMS or SIS platforms?

Yes. We have experience integrating with popular learning management systems like Canvas and Moodle, as well as student information systems such as PowerSchool. Our APIs and middleware ensure seamless data flow and single sign-on capabilities.
